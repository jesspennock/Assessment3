Career Goals and Ambitions

First and foremost I would like to become a really good software engineer. My plan is to specialize in Web Development. After getting a few years of experience as an individual contributor, I would love the opportunity to transition to project and people management. I know that managers with a strong technical background are a great asset to teams and companies.

In my "wish-list-maybe-someday-goals" I have an idea to start a non profit that employees software engineers on the Autism Spectrum. My brother and my son both have been diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder. They both have the capabilities to be an incredible asset to a technical team someday, but the communication difficulties that frequently accompany these types of diagnoses are a real roadblock to finding success in the work place. I feel like a software shop with a balance of communication mentors and talented, neurodivergent engineers could be a really amazing thing!
